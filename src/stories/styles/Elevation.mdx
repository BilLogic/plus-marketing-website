# Elevation & Depth

We rely on subtle shadows, rings, and glassmorphism to create depth.

## Tokens
- **Base (Flat)**: `shadow-sm` for standard container cards.
- **Floating (Premium)**: `shadow-xl shadow-primary/10` combined with `ring-1 ring-border`. Applies to hovered items or important callouts.
- **Glassmorphism**: When laying over images or gradients, use `bg-background/80 backdrop-blur-md`.
