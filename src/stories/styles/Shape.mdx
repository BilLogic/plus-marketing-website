# Shape & Border Radii

We use rounded corners to soften the UI and feel modern, but restrict it to a specific scale.

## Scale
- **Inner Elements (Buttons, Tags)**: `rounded-md` (6px) or `rounded-full` (for pills).
- **Cards & Containers**: `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl` for a friendlier, modern marketing aesthetic.
- **Hero Containers**: Occasionally `rounded-[2rem]` or higher depending on the component's scale to match the aesthetic direction.
