# Typography

## Font Families
- **Display/Headings**: `Outfit` or `Geist Sans`
- **Body**: `Inter` or `Geist Sans`

## Heading Hierarchy
- **H1 (Hero)**: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold tracking-tight`
- **H2 (Section Header)**: `text-3xl md:text-5xl font-bold tracking-tight`
- **H3 (Card Header)**: `text-xl md:text-2xl font-bold`

## Body Text
- **Large (Lead)**: `text-lg md:text-xl text-muted-foreground`
- **Standard**: `text-base text-foreground leading-relaxed (150%+)`
- **Small (Caption)**: `text-sm text-muted-foreground`

> Rule: Limit paragraph widths to optimal reading lengths using `max-w-prose` or similar utilities.
